Quick Fitness Tips

  • Stay hydrated: Drink at least 8 glasses of water a day.
  • Consistency is key: Aim for regular exercise, even if it's just 30 minutes a day.
  • Balance your diet: Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, proteins, and whole grains.
  • Get enough sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours per night for optimal recovery.
