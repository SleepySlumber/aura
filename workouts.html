Workout Plans

Beginner Full Body Workout

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bent-over rows: 3 sets of 10 reps (use dumbbells or water bottles)
  • Planks: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds

Cardio Routine

30 minutes of brisk walking, jogging, or cycling 3-4 times a week.
